# SignDeployer — Go-To-Market Plan: 100 Sign-Ups by July 1

**Window:** June 7 → July 1 (24 days). **Goal:** 100 account sign-ups.
**Product:** Finds the highest-visibility corners for open-house directional signs by
analyzing the real road network + the routes buyers drive to a listing, then outputs a
route-ordered sign plan in seconds.
**Pricing already built:** Individual $49/yr or $7/mo · Brokerage $499/yr (50 seats + branding).

---

## 1. The core insight that makes this winnable

You are NOT selling software. You are selling **more bodies through the front door of an open
house**. Every agent already knows the pain: they buy 6–10 directional signs, then drive around
guessing where to stick them, often missing the one corner where 80% of traffic actually turns.
Bad sign placement = empty open house = no leads = no commission.

The whole pitch is one sentence: **"Stop guessing where your open house signs go — get a
route-optimized plan in 30 seconds so more buyers actually find the house."**

Timing is perfect: **summer is peak open-house season** and there are 3.5 weekends inside your
window (Jun 13–14, 20–21, 27–28). Each weekend is a live demo opportunity.

## 2. The launch offer (this is what hits 100, not the $49 price)

Asking strangers for $49 cold will not get you 100 sign-ups in 24 days. Lead with a
**free, no-credit-card founding offer** and convert to paid later.

> ### 🏆 "Founding Agent" Offer (cap it at 100 on purpose)
> - **Free through the entire 2026 summer season** (no card required to start).
> - First 100 agents lock a **Founding rate of $29/year for life** when they convert.
> - In exchange you ask for ONE thing: a 1-line testimonial + a photo of their open-house
>   sign plan if it helped. (This fuels all your social proof.)

Why this works:
- **Scarcity** ("only 100 spots") creates urgency that matches your deadline.
- **No card** removes the #1 signup-killer and gets you to 100 fast.
- **Lifetime $29 lock** rewards early believers and still converts to revenue.
- You harvest **testimonials + screenshots** that make weeks 3–4 (and all future selling) easy.

Set up a simple counter you can reference everywhere: *"37 of 100 founding spots claimed."*

## 3. Who you're targeting (ICP) — in priority order

1. **Solo / small-team residential agents who do weekly open houses.** Newer agents (0–5 yrs)
   especially — they rely on open houses for leads and have the least systematized process.
2. **Team leads** (5–30 agents) — one yes = many seats, and they love giving their team an edge.
3. **Brokerage office managers** — your path to the $499 plan; one office demo can produce 20+
   signups at once.

Where they congregate (your hunting grounds):
- Zillow / Realtor.com / Homes.com agent directories (public emails + phone + headshots)
- Facebook groups: "Lab Coat Agents", "Real Estate Agents Network", local "[City] Realtors"
- Instagram: agents post listings + open houses daily with hashtags
- Local brokerage offices (KW, eXp, Coldwell Banker, RE/MAX, Compass, independents)

## 5. Channel playbook

### A) Cold email (your volume engine → ~45 sign-ups)

**Build the list (Days 1–3):** Pull 1,500 agent emails from Zillow/Realtor.com directories in
your target metros. Use a scraper (Apollo.io, Instantly's lead finder, PhantomBuster, or a VA on
Fiverr/Upwork for ~$100). Capture: first name, brokerage, city.

**Sending setup (do this FIRST — Day 1):**
- Buy a **separate domain** for cold email (e.g. `getsigndeployer.com`) so you never risk the
  deliverability of `signdeployer.com`. Forward it to your main site.
- Set up **SPF, DKIM, DMARC**. Warm the inbox for a few days (Instantly/Smartlead auto-warmup).
- Send **30–50/day per inbox**, ramping up. Use 2–3 inboxes to safely hit ~150/day total.
- Tool: **Instantly.ai** or **Smartlead** (~$37–97/mo) handles sending, warmup, sequencing.

**3-email sequence** (templates in §7). Keep them short, plain-text, one clear ask: *try the free
demo* (no signup friction) → then *claim a founding spot*.

### B) Instagram (your trust + inbound engine → ~30 sign-ups)

**Set up the account (Day 1):** `@signdeployer`
- Bio: *"📍 Put your open house signs where buyers actually drive. Free route-optimized sign
  plans for agents. 100 founding spots → link below."*
- Link: a simple landing link (or Linktree) → live demo + founding signup.
- Profile pic: your logo (the black pin mark from the site).

**Content (post 1×/day, mostly Reels):** Real estate agents live on IG Reels. Ideas:
1. Screen-recording: type an address → watch the sign plan generate. (Your single best asset —
   it's visual and instantly "gets it".)
2. "I placed my open house signs wrong for 3 years. Here's the fix." (story hook)
3. Before/after: random sign placement vs. route-optimized.
4. "POV: it's Saturday 9am and you're driving around guessing where signs go 😩"
5. A 30-sec tip on open-house traffic + soft mention of the tool.
6. Testimonials/screenshots from founding agents (weeks 2–4).

**DM outreach (the real driver — 30–40/day):** Search hashtags **#openhouse #realtorlife
#newlisting #[city]realtor**, find agents who *just posted an open house*, and DM them. Script in
§8. This is timely and personal — far higher conversion than cold email.

**Accounts/hashtags to engage daily:** #realtor #realtorlife #openhouse #realestateagent
#newlisting #justlisted #[yourcity]realestate #labcoatagents. Comment genuinely on 10
agent posts/day to warm them before DMing.

### C) Facebook groups (community trust → ~20 sign-ups)

Join 10–15 agent groups (Lab Coat Agents, Real Estate Agents Network, local realtor groups).
**Do NOT spam links.** Spend Days 1–4 commenting helpfully. Then post a **value-first** post
(template in §7): share a genuinely useful open-house traffic tip, mention you built a free tool,
offer it to anyone who comments "SIGN". DM the commenters the link. Repeat in a different group
every 2–3 days.

### D) Brokerage office demos (highest leverage → ~25 sign-ups)

One 10-minute demo at a Tuesday sales meeting can sign up a whole office. Email/call office
managers (template in §7), offer to give the whole team free founding access. Bring a live demo
of a listing in THEIR area. This is also your direct path to the **$499 Brokerage plan**.

### E) Referral loop (compounds weeks 2–4)

In your onboarding email and in-app, ask every founding agent: *"Know another agent who does open
houses? Send them this — if 3 join, your $29 founding rate becomes free for life."* Free signups
that beget signups.

## 6. Day-by-day 24-day execution calendar

### Week 0 — Setup (Jun 7–9)
- **Jun 7:** Register cold-email domain; set up SPF/DKIM/DMARC; start inbox warmup. Create
  `@signdeployer` IG. Set up Instantly/Smartlead. Spin up the "Founding Agent" landing/counter.
- **Jun 8:** Start scraping 1,500 agent emails (or brief a VA). Write the 3-email sequence into
  the tool. Join 15 Facebook groups + start commenting. Film 5 IG Reels in one batch.
- **Jun 9:** Finalize list segments by city. Schedule first week of IG posts. Draft brokerage
  outreach list (20 local offices + manager contacts).

### Week 1 — Launch outreach (Jun 10–16) — *target ~25 signups*
- Start cold email sending (ramp 50→150/day). Send 30 IG DMs/day. First Facebook value post.
- Email 20 brokerage managers; book demos for the following week.
- **Jun 13–14 (open-house weekend):** DM agents posting open houses in real time — "saw your open
  house this weekend, want a free sign plan for it?" Highest-intent moment of the week.

### Week 2 — Scale what works (Jun 17–23) — *target ~35 signups*
- Send email sequence #2 to non-openers (new subject line). Keep DMs at 40/day.
- Run 2–3 brokerage office demos. Post testimonials from week-1 founders to IG/FB.
- Second + third Facebook value posts in different groups.
- **Jun 20–21 weekend:** repeat real-time open-house DM blitz.

### Week 3 — Push to 100 (Jun 24–30) — *target ~40 signups*
- Email sequence #3 with the **scarcity close**: "Only X founding spots left, closing July 1."
- Flip every IG post + story to the countdown. DM everyone who engaged but didn't sign up.
- Run remaining brokerage demos. Activate the referral ask with existing founders.
- **Jun 27–28 weekend:** final open-house DM blitz.

### Jul 1 — Close
- "Founding 100 is full / closing today" post. Convert free founders toward the $29 lock.
- Tally results, collect testimonials, plan the paid-conversion phase.
