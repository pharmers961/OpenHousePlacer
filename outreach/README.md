# Outreach List — How to use it

`top-agents-list.csv` is a **verified seed list**: every email here was pulled from the team's
own website or a public listing (not guessed). Source links are at the bottom of this file.

## Honest read on this list

You asked for "top by homes sold." I included some of the biggest teams in the country (Robert
Slack, Loken Group, Jills Zeder). Be aware of the trade-off:

- **Mega-teams** (`info@` inboxes) are *gatekept by admins* and have ops staff who already handle
  open-house logistics → **lower reply rate**, but one yes = the **$499 brokerage plan**.
- **Direct agent / team-lead emails** (Harmes, Team Price, the Compass Dallas agents, LIV
  Sotheby's, Nashville's Nik) are marked **ICP_Fit = High** — these people *personally* run open
  houses and read their own inbox. **Start here.**

## The 100-signup math doesn't come from a hand-built list

15 verified contacts won't get you to 100. This CSV is a **quality seed** to test your messaging
and land a few flagship logos/testimonials. For the *volume* (1,500 contacts) you need a scraped
list. Two clean, compliant ways to build it:

1. **Apollo.io** — filter Industry = Real Estate, Title = Realtor/Agent/Broker, by metro. Exports
   verified emails. Free tier gives a few hundred; paid ~$49/mo for thousands.
2. **A VA on Upwork/Fiverr (~$100)** — brief them: "Pull name + email + brokerage + city for
   1,500 residential agents in [metros] from public brokerage sites and agent directories." This
   sidesteps Zillow's scraping block (Zillow hides emails behind contact forms on purpose).

> ⚠️ **Zillow note:** Zillow does **not** expose agent emails and its ToS forbids scraping. The
> reliable public sources are the agents' own brokerage/team sites, RealTrends/America's Best
> rankings (for *who* the top producers are), and directory aggregators — which is exactly how
> this seed list was built.

## Before you send anything (deliverability + law)

- **Do NOT blast cold email from your personal Gmail.** It tanks your domain reputation and Gmail
  will throttle you. Use the warmed sending domain + Instantly/Smartlead setup from
  `GO-TO-MARKET.md`.
- **CAN-SPAM compliance** (this is legal B2B outreach if you follow it): use a truthful subject &
  "from", include a physical mailing address in the footer, and give a real one-click unsubscribe.
- For the **High-fit named individuals**, skip the blast — send a *personalized* one-to-one email
  (reference a recent listing of theirs). Reply rates are far higher.

## Suggested sequence

1. **This week:** personally email the ~9 High-fit contacts (use Cold Email #1 in your Gmail
   drafts, personalized). Goal: 2-3 flagship founding agents + testimonials.
2. **Next week:** build the 1,500-contact scraped list (Apollo or VA).
3. **Load** that list into Instantly/Smartlead and run the 3-email sequence already drafted.
