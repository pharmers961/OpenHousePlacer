Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

This policy explains what SignDeployer ("we") collects, why, and what we do with it. The short version: we collect what's needed to run a paid sign-placement tool — your email, your subscription status, and the listings you choose to save — and we don't sell any of it.

1. What we collect

DataWhenWhy
Email addressWhen you sign in (one-time email link)Your account identity and sign-in
Name, company name, phone, team sizeIf you buy a Brokerage planSetting up your team account and contacting you about it
Subscription statusFrom our payment processorKnowing whether to unlock the app
Saved listing addressesWhen you click "Save this open house"Syncing your saved listings across your devices
Searched addressesWhen you run a searchSent to Mapbox to compute the sign plan; recent results are also cached in your own browser
IP address (hashed)When you use the free demoRate-limiting to prevent abuse; we store a one-way hash, not the IP itself
Company logo/brandingIf a brokerage owner uploads oneDisplaying your branding to your team

Payment details: handled entirely by Stripe. Your card number never touches our servers.

2. What we don't do

  • We don't sell or rent your personal information.
  • We don't use advertising trackers or third-party ad networks.
  • We don't read your saved listings for any purpose other than showing them to you.

3. Where your data lives

Account data and saved listings are stored with Supabase (our database provider). The site is hosted on Netlify. Payments are processed by Stripe. Address searches are processed by Mapbox to compute results. Sign-in emails are delivered via our email provider. Each of these processors has its own security and privacy program.

4. Cookies and local storage

We don't use advertising cookies. Your browser's local storage holds your sign-in session (via Supabase), your recent searches and settings, and a cache of results so repeat searches are instant. Clearing your browser storage removes these; saved listings remain in your account.

5. How long we keep data

Account data is kept while your account exists. Saved listings are kept until you delete them. Demo rate-limit hashes are short-lived operational records. Billing records are retained as required for tax and accounting purposes.

6. Your choices

  • Access or delete your data: email hello@signdeployer.com and we'll handle it. Deleting your account removes your profile and saved listings.
  • Cancel billing: Account → Manage billing, any time.
  • Team members: if a brokerage added you, the brokerage owner can remove you, or you can ask us to detach your account.

7. Security

Sign-in uses one-time email links (no passwords to leak). Access to paid features is verified server-side on every request. Database access is restricted with row-level security so each user can only read their own records. Secrets and API keys are stored in server environment variables, not in the site's code.

8. Children

The Service is for working real-estate professionals and is not directed at children under 16.

9. Changes

If we materially change this policy, we'll update this page and the date above.

10. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: hello@signdeployer.com
